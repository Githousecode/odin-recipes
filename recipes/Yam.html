About Yam

Yam is a common staple food made by boiling yam slices until soft. It is filling, slightly sweet, and often eaten with sauce, stew or egg.

Ingredients for preparing Yam

  • Yam
  • Water
  • Salt or Sugar

Steps in preparing Yam

  1. Peel the yam and cut into pieces.
  2. Wash the pieces properly.
  3. Put in a pot and add water.
  4. Add a little salt or sugar.
  5. Boil until the yam is soft.
  6. Drain the water.
  7. Serve with sauce or stew.
