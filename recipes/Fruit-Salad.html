About Fruit Salad

Fruit salad is a simple and healthy dish made by mixing different fresh fruit like apples, bananas, graapes, oranges, and watermelon. It usually served cold and sometimes combined with yogurt, honey or cream for extra taste.

Ingredients for preparing Fruit Salad

  • Apple
  • Banana
  • Orange
  • Grapes
  • Watermelon
  • Pineapple
  • Mango
  • Yogurt or cream
  • Honey or cream

Steps on how to prepare Fruit Salad

  1. Wash all the fruits properly.
  2. Peel and cut the fruits into small pieces.
  3. Put all the fruits in a clean bowl.
  4. Mix gently so they combine well.
  5. Add yogurt, cream, or honey if you want extra taste.
  6. Chill in the fridge for few minutes.
  7. Serve cold and enjoy.
