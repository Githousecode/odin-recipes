About Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a simple and delicious pasta dish made by cooking spaghetti and mixing it with sauce, vegetables, and sometimes meat or fish. it is soft, flavorful, and filling.

Ingredients for preparing Spaghetti

  • Spaghetti
  • Water
  • Vegetables oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes or tomato paste
  • Pepper
  • Carrots
  • Green pepper
  • Chicken, beef, or fish
  • Salt
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Curry and thyme

Steps for preparing Spaghetti

  1. Boil water and cook the spaghetti, then drain.
  2. Heat oil in a pot and fry onions and garlic.
  3. Add Tomatoesor paste and cook for few minutes.
  4. Add chicken, beef, or fish.
  5. Add vegetables and stir.
  6. Add the cooked spaghetti and mix.
  7. Add salt, seasoning, curry, and thyme.
  8. Stir well, cook for few minutes and serve.
