About Moi-Moi

Moi-moi is a soft, steamed bean pudding made from blended beans, oil, and spices. It is tasty, smooth, and often eaten with rice, bread or pap.

Ingredients for preparing Moi-Moi

  • Beans (peeled)
  • Vegetable oil or palm oil
  • Pepper
  • Onion
  • Crayfish
  • Fish or egg
  • Salt
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Water

Steps for preparing Moi-Moi

  1. Wash and peel the beans.
  2. Blend the beans with pepper and onion until smooth.
  3. Pour into a bowl and add oil, salt, Seasoning, and water.
  4. Mix well until smooth.
  5. Add fish or egg.
  6. Pour the mixture into container or leaves.
  7. steam for about 30-40 minutes.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy.
