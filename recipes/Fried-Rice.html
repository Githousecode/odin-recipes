About Fried-Rice

Fried rice is a popular dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with a mix of ingredients to create a flavorful, slightly dry, and well seasoned meal.

Ingredients for Fried-Rice

  • Rice
  • vegetable oil
  • Carrots
  • Peas
  • Sweet corn
  • Green beans
  • Green pepper
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Chicken or shrimp
  • Soy sauce
  • Curry powder
  • Thyme
  • Salt
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Black pepper
  • Spring onions

Steps for preparing Fried Rice

  1. Cook the rice and allow it to cool.
  2. Heat oil in a pan.
  3. Add chopped onions and garlic, then fry briefly.
  4. Add your chicken, shrimp etc and cook well.
  5. Add vegetables and stir-fry for a few minutes.
  6. Add the cooked rice and mix everything toegther.
  7. Seasoned with soy sauce, curry, thyme, slat, and pepper.
  8. Stir-fry for 3-5 minutes.
  9. Add spring Onions and serve.
