About Noodles

Noodles is a quick and tasty dish made by cooking instant noodles with water, season, and sometimes vegetables or protein. it is soft, flavorful, and easy to prepapre.

Ingredients for preparing Noodles

  • Instant noodles
  • Water
  • Noodles Seasoning
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Carrots
  • Green peas
  • Egg or chicken

Steps for preparing Noodles

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Add the noodles and cook for a few minutes.
  3. Add the Seasoning and stir well.
  4. Add onion, pepper, and vegetables.
  5. Add egg or chicken.
  6. Cook for 2-3 more minutes.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy.
