About Macaroni

Macaroni is a simple and tasty pasta dish made by cooking macaroni with vegetables, sauce, and sometimes meat or fish. It is soft, flavorful, and easy to prepare.

Ingredient for Macaroni

  • Macaroni
  • Vegetable oil
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes or tomatoe pasta
  • Carrots
  • Green peas
  • Green pepper
  • Chicken, fish or beef
  • Salt
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Curry and thyme

    • Steps for preparing Macaroni

    1. Boil the macaroni and drain the water.
    2. Heat oil in a pot and fry onions and garlic.
    3. Add tomatoes or pasta and cook for a few minutes.
    4. Add your chicken, fish or beef and cook well.
    5. Add vegetables and stir.
    6. Put in the cooked macaroni and mix.
    7. Add salt, seasoning, curry, and thyme.
    8. Stir well, cook for a few minutes, and serve.
